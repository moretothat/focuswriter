# How to Sell FocusWriter for $20

This guide walks you through setting up sales for FocusWriter with minimal effort.

## Quickest Path: Gumroad (15 minutes setup)

### Step 1: Build Your App

Double-click `BUILD_AND_INSTALL.command` in the FocusWriter folder. Wait for it to complete.

### Step 2: Prepare Your Files

1. Navigate to `FocusWriter/dist/`
2. Find the DMG file (e.g., `FocusWriter-1.0.0.dmg`)
3. Create a ZIP: Right-click the DMG > Compress

### Step 3: Create Gumroad Account

1. Go to [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com)
2. Sign up for free
3. Complete your profile

### Step 4: Create Product

1. Click "New Product"
2. Fill in details:

**Name:** FocusWriter - Lock Yourself In and Write

**Price:** $20

**Description (copy this):**
```
FocusWriter locks your Mac to a distraction-free writing environment until your timer runs out.

No escape. No distractions. Just writing.

FEATURES:
- Full-screen lockdown mode that blocks other apps
- Customizable session length (1 min to 8 hours)
- Beautiful minimal interface
- Live word count
- Auto-save every 3 seconds
- Progress bar showing time remaining
- Emergency exit (requires typing a specific phrase)
- Export your work when done

Perfect for:
- Writers who struggle with distractions
- Students working on papers
- Anyone who needs to focus

macOS 10.15+ required.

One-time purchase. No subscription. No account needed.
```

**Upload:** Your ZIP file containing the DMG

### Step 5: Add Cover Image

Create a simple image (1280x720) showing:
- The app interface
- Text: "FocusWriter - $20"
- "Lock in. Write. No distractions."

Use Canva.com (free) to make this quickly.

### Step 6: Publish and Share

1. Click Publish
2. Copy your product link
3. Share on:
   - Twitter/X
   - Reddit (r/writing, r/productivity, r/macapps)
   - Product Hunt
   - Hacker News
   - Writing forums

## Pricing Strategy

$20 is good because:
- Low enough for impulse buys
- High enough to signal quality
- No subscription = easy sell
- Similar apps charge $20-40

Consider adding:
- Launch discount: $15 for first week
- Bundle with other productivity tools

## Expected Sales

Realistic expectations for a solo launch:
- Week 1: 5-20 sales ($100-400)

With good marketing/viral post: 10x these numbers

## Support

Expect minimal support needs since the app is simple. Common questions:

Q: "It won't open / developer not verified"
A: Right-click > Open > Open Anyway

Q: "I'm stuck in a session"
A: Emergency exit: type "I understand this defeats the purpose of focused writing"

Q: "Can I get a refund?"
A: Your call. Gumroad handles refunds if you enable them.
