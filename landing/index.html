The Problem

Willpower is finite.
Your environment isn't.

You've told yourself a thousand times that today is the day you'll finally write. You open your laptop, ready to create. And then... you check your email. Just for a second. Then a quick look at the news. Before you know it, two hours have passed and your document is still blank.

Every time you resist the urge to switch tabs, you deplete a little more willpower. The problem isn't that you lack discipline. The problem is that your environment is designed to steal your attention.

The solution isn't to try harder. It's to remove the possibility of distraction entirely.

FAQ

Questions

What if I have a real emergency?

FocusWriter includes an emergency exit. Type the phrase "I understand this defeats the purpose of focused writing" to unlock early. It's intentionally inconvenient - easy enough for a genuine emergency, annoying enough that you won't use it to check Twitter.

What macOS versions are supported?

FocusWriter works on macOS 10.15 (Catalina) and later, including Apple Silicon Macs. Windows support is coming soon.

What if the app crashes?

Your work is auto-saved every few seconds to a recovery file. If anything goes wrong, your words will be waiting for you when you reopen the app.

Can I use my own text editor?

FocusWriter is a complete writing environment - you write directly in the app. When your session ends, you can export to a .txt or .md file and continue editing elsewhere.

How much does it cost?

FocusWriter is free for now while it's in early release. If that changes down the road, you'll keep what you've already downloaded.
